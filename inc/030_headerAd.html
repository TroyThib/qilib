Eat your way to healthy hair?

Did you know that too little protein in your diet can damage healthy hair? Or that it can hinder your body’s ability to build new hair follicles?

A protein-deficient diet can lead to significant hair loss in 2 to 3 months. So it’s more important than ever to ensure you’re eating a well-balanced, protein-rich diet.
Sleep on it.

Did you know that 80% of men and 40% of women experience hair loss or that 1 in 4 suffer from some form of sleep problem? Coincidence? Our bodies need sleep to function. It’s precisely when our systems recharge with electrolytes and replenish energy. If we don’t get enough sleep at night, our immune system, due to lack of nutrients, becomes weak. This can cause roots to become weak which can lead to hair loss and balding. So, make sure to get your 8 hours!
